## DevOps Engineer
- Focuses on improving how software is built, tested, deployed, and operated.
- Works between developers and operations to remove friction.
- Owns delivery pipelines, automation, and environment consistency.

## Site Reliability Engineer (SRE)
- Focuses on system reliability and availability in production.
- Uses engineering approaches to operations problems.
- Owns SLIs, SLOs, error budgets, and incident response.

## Platform Engineer
- Builds internal platforms that developers use to deploy and run services.
- Abstracts infrastructure complexity behind self-service tools.
- Focuses on developer experience and standardisation.

## Why these roles exist
- Modern systems are too complex for manual operations.
- Speed without reliability breaks businesses.
- These roles exist to balance delivery speed, stability, and scale.
